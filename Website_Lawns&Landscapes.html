ABOUT US:

Lawns and Landscapes Limited is a private company that deals in horticulture, real estate and construction, standardized street and house naming/numbering, among others, is a setup that is making a difference in the industry. The Company is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA 2020). In spite of her short period of operation, the company is doing well in contributing to greening. This is done through series of researches and developments, sensitization, consultancy services and partnerships with relevant firms and organisations to bring about green solutions. We specialize in providing tailored greening services at affordable prices. We are sure to deliver on bespoke services that are tailored to meet specific client needs. Our quality management standard guarantees excellent internal management and operational processes that assures customer satisfaction and ultimately, customer loyalty. Lawns and Landscapes Ltd is poised to build on a steady growth that is based on quality delivery and customer satisfaction. At its present level, the company is well placed, operates on global standards and is committed to providing world class services to specific customer requirements.

Scope of Work

Our SCOPE of activities include:

  • Horticultural Beautification
    •

    Floriculture, olericulture, pomology, ornamental horticulture, etc. Plus Buying, selling, exporting, importing all kinds of flowers, plants and ornamental goods.

  • Real Estate and Construction
    •

    Landscaping and beautification, providing construction services- from planning to erection to improvement to repairs, maintenance and also demolition of any construction.

  • Naming and Road Marking
    •

    Standardized street naming and house numbering, road markings, directional and notice signs and safety signs.

  • Street/Traffic Lights and Security
    •

    Procurement, fabrication, securing, repairing and maintaining streets and traffic lights, CCTV cameras, street security systems and alarming systems, in whole or part.

  • General Cartography
    •

    Updating and charting maps and other cartographic services.

  • Water Engineering Water Resource Management
    •

    Management of artificial reservoirs/lakes, fountains, bioindication/remediation, environmental auditing/accounting, ecotoxicology, ecolabelling, water analysis, erosion control, waste management, etc.

  • Our services are however not limited to the above listed.
